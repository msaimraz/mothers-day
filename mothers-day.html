A Letter to Every Mother

Dear Mom, we know you never asked for a thank you. You never expected a standing ovation for the million small acts of love you performed every single day. But today, we see you.

We see the mornings you woke up before the sun just to pack lunches. The nights you stayed up worrying. The tears you hid so we could feel strong. The dreams you quietly set aside so ours could take flight.

You are the reason we believe in magic, in kindness, in the power of a warm hug to fix almost anything. You are our first love story, our greatest teacher, and our forever safe place.

Thank you for everything — the seen and the unseen, the spoken and the silent. You are extraordinary, even on the days you feel ordinary. We love you beyond words, beyond time, beyond measure.

With all our love, always 💕
